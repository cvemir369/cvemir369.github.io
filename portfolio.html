TheraSync

TheraSync - Connecting Users with Therapists Using AI.

Revolutionizes mental health care by using AI to connect users with the right therapists. Users start by answering questions to generate an initial AI analysis, then track their progress through daily journaling, which the AI evaluates to provide insights and improve therapist matching. Therapists also register, detailing their expertise, and are matched with clients based on AI-driven insights. This personalized, efficient approach makes mental health support more accessible, effective, and tailored to individual needs.

TrendVibe eCommerce

TrendVibe - eCommerce online store React app.

This project is an example of fully functional online store built using React for the frontend and powered by the FakeStore API to fetch product data. It showcases a modern, responsive, and user-friendly interface for browsing products, adding them to the cart, and simulating a seamless shopping experience. Browse a wide range of products fetched from the FakeStore API, including categories like electronics, jewelry, men's clothing, and women's clothing. View detailed information about each product, including images, descriptions, prices, and ratings. Add and remove products from your cart, with real-time updates to the total price. The app is fully responsive and works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Clean and intuitive user interface designed to enhance the shopping experience.

Pokémon: Battle Game

Pokémon: Battle Game - Battle the CPU with your favorite Pokémon.

Pokémon: Battle Game (Web App) is an online battle simulator where players log in, select their Pokémon from a predefined roster, and face off against a randomly generated CPU team. The outcome of each battle is determined by the Pokémon's stats (like HP, Attack, Defense, etc.) and a special fight formula that calculates damage, type advantages, and critical hits. Build your strongest team, leverage type matchups, and outsmart the CPU to claim victory! Perfect for quick, strategic Pokémon battles in your browser.
